Irungattukottai

Irungattukottai is an important industrial town located in the Kanchipuram district of Tamil Nadu, India. It is widely known as a major automobile and manufacturing hub and is often called the "Detroit of South India."

The town hosts several automobile manufacturing companies and industrial parks. It is well connected to Chennai by road and plays a significant role in the economic growth of the region while maintaining a mix of modern development and traditional Tamil culture.
